The metal master Fabrication was established on June 13, 2003, on the welfare of our citizen people and moreover to provided the door to door services erstwhile National Technical Training Authority (NTTA) and the Department of Employment and Labour. The NTTA was established in May 1999 with the mandate to develop a vocational education and training (VET) system in order to produce skilled and productive workforce for the kingdom. To achieve its objectives, the NTTA was required to plan, coordinate, regulate, support, and where needed, implement vocational education and training.
